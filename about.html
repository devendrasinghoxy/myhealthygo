Know About us

We aim to be the best in the business of “Direct Marketing” - keeping in mind the growth potential and the beneficial aspects of this business. To reach out to common people with the advantage of this business and to train them as to how they can benefit from it. To pass on the ambitious thoughts and the benefits we have in mind for all of us, we have launched ‘Healthygo Multitrade L.L.P.' in the market. Fulfilling the mission of “Direct Marketing” both for the end user and the manufacturer is possible for increased earnings to both, and this has been proven.

We visualize this possibility through this mission and we would be making an attempt to reach out to the masses through ‘Healthygo Multitrade L.L.P.’ wherein the masses would know our excellent business plans and the quality of our products. ‘Healthygo Multitrade L.L.P.’ would give an opportunity to the common man to do business of world class products and also have enormous earning potential.

Mission & Goal

Network Marketing has been recognized as one of the best businesses existing today. But, unfortunately the way it has been represented has created wrong conceptions in the mind of the common man of the true and wonderful business potential of network marketing. The common man thinks that the companies doing ‘network marketing’ want to make quick money, exchanging money for cheap products. ‘Healthygo Multitrade L.L.P.’ wants to make a determined and an honest attempt to change this misconception and to further reinforce the mission, propagate the truth and the true value awareness amongst common people for this type of business.

Along with the best products and marketing tools, ‘Healthygo Multitrade L.L.P.’ wants that the investments made by the end users gets really appreciated and the sincere efforts put in by our representatives get recognition. ‘Healthygo Multitrade L.L.P.’ wants to prove that the hard work put in by our trained members of the ‘Healthygo Multitrade L.L.P.’ family so their hard work becomes more profitable and would strengthen their position in our society. This is the goal and purpose of ‘Healthygo Multitrade L.L.P.’

Success Systems & Techniques (S.S.T.)

S.S.T. will be one of the sister Concern Company of ‘Healthygo Multitrdae’. This company will develop models of business development training, leadership development training, and commando training by concentrating on the strength of each individual providing continuous guidance through close monitoring on a ‘one-to-one’ basis. In future, through ‘Healthygo Multitrdae’ will take up programs such as seminars, mega seminars, actual demonstrations, technical seminars, training modules – all of which shall be under the umbrella of SST.

We propose to create a hard core group of people from various fields but out of people who are members of ‘Healthygo Multitrdae’, to translate hidden energies of our family members into power centers. This hard core group shall formulate and guide all the activities and plans with prime focus on the future – evaluate and examine all programmes and their contents – before ‘Healthygo Multitrdae’ actually places them before the family members of ‘Healthygo Multitrdae’.

SST will comprise of eminent persons from different fields who are totally committed and who are prepared to work day in and day out for the sole purpose of promoting the mission ‘Healthygo Multitrdae’ has in mind.
